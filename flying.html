PANDA

Step 1) Fold and unfold the paper in half both ways.

Step 2) Fold the side corners to the centre.

Step 3) Fold both sides sides to the centre.

Step 4) Open the paper back up.

Step 5) Fold the side over to the right along the dotted line. This is an existing crease.

Step 6) Fold the top layer of paper out along the dotted line.

Step 7) Repeat steps 5 and 6 on the other side.

Step 8) Fold the paper up along the dotted line.

Step 9) Fold the paper back down along the dotted line.

Step 10) Fold the top part of the paper down along the dotted line.

Step 11) Fold the paper back up along the dotted line.

Step 12) Fold the little corners along the dotted lines and then unfold them. Youâ€™ll use these creases in the next step.

Step 13) Fold the paper out along the dotted lines and use the creases from the last step. Flatten everything.

Step 14) Fold the paper up along the dotted line.

Step 15) Turn the paper over.

Step 16) Fold the corners up along the dotted lines.

Step 17) Fold the paper back down along the dotted lines.

Step 18) Fold the paper up along the dotted line pulling the paper out from underneath.

Step 19) Make a Pleat Fold along the dotted line.
